## Little yaps

we built a fake 2016 internet inside a fake chrome browser so you can search "ChatGPT" and watch google have a full crisis about it. google, youtube, twitter, everything and anything all pixel-accurate, all stuck in 2016, all deeply confused by anything you actually care about. 

Built with chatgpt for prompting, claudi (claude-kun) for code gen, and codex when claudi hit her usage limit and abandoned us mid-hackathon.

---

## Easter eggs

- **dialup boot screen** — 56K modem startup sequence before you can do anything. you deserve this.
- **popup hell** — 30% chance per navigation. draggable. clicking any button spawns more. yes, even the X.
- **harambe memorial** — search "harambe" anywhere. fullscreen. includes the quote. we are not sorry.
- **clippy** — 5% chance per page. both buttons say "go away" and "don't help."
- **google+ notification** — 0 notifications. always 0.
- **future years** — search "2017" and google suggests "did you mean: 2016?" and asks "how does time work?"
- **no results** — IE-styled yellow box that tells you to "wait a few years and try again."
